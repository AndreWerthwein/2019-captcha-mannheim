Open Call

WHAT TO SUBMIT?

You’re completely free in the choice of style and design of your work. From graphic and illustration to drawing, photography, editorial or experimental — everything is appreciated. So you’re free in method and media — the only requirement is the topic NOISE.


HOW TO SUBMIT?

Create a PDF (max. 2 MB), in which you present your work. It should contain images of your work and outline the concept with a short statement.

Send in your Work!
ENTRY DEADLINE: AUGUST, 15TH

We are looking forward to seeing your work!

Please Consider

We are not able to bear the costs of the packaging or shipping of three-dimensional projects.

The PDF should not be bigger than 2 MB. If required, we will request a separate print file.
