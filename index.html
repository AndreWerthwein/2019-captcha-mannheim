September 03 — September 16

Initiated and organized by students of the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim the CAPTCHA Design Festival takes place for the 5th time this summer. The workshops and the symposium revolve around this year’s topic DIMENSION. Together with our participating students reputable guests from Design and Art will develop innovative and new ways of designing. The results will be presented in a final one-week exhibition.

Symposium

Every beginning is in need of information! So get ready for many interesting lectures given on the first day of the festival where this year’s topic and central questions will be discussed. Our lecturers will show their work of Design and Art and shed light on the theoretical components of the topic DIMENSION.

Workshops

The workshops are intended for up to 60 design students and will take place in the facilities of the zeitraumexit from the 4th to the 7th of September. Our four guest designers of a variety of fields will realize the topic DIMENSION in different ways. In each workshop the participants and the workshop lead will develop their own projects and work together experimentally.

Präsens Büro Editorial/Typography
Dominik Rinnhofer Projection/Interaction
Jonathan Castro Graphic Design
Simone Cihlar Concept Design

Exhibition

After the four days of workshops all results will be shown and celebrated for one week. The exhibition takes place at the Kunstverein Mannheim from the 8th to the 16th of September.

Simone Cihlar

Simone Cihlar is a freelance graphic designer and happy human being based in Berlin.

Her work is mainly focused on the music scene and through the years she collaborated with many musicians and record labels, such as Jakarta Record, Habib Funk and Tom Misch. Especially through her collages and the collaboration with Anderson .Paak she became known to a larger audience. Simone will help you understand the connections between music and design within her workshop. The goal is to work with different musicians to create new styles: from merchandise to artwork; you will be working in close connection with the respective artist. It would be fantastic if the results of the workshop could be realised or even published. This would allow for an authentic experience, that is of the collaboration between music industry and customers. Additionally it will add to your own portfolio.

Workshop and symposium.

Präsens Büro

Präsens Büro is a Swiss graphic design studio based in Zurich and Lucerne. The studio, consisting of Simon Rüegg and Raphael Schoen, was founded in late 2012. Interested in a wide range of projects from editorial design to visual identities, their clients mostly have their origin in the cultural field. Besides commissioned work Präsens Büro works on self-initiated projects and is part of the design team of zweikommasieben Magazin.

Rules Over Style: The exploration of a rule based approach in editorial design. The result will be a fully readable magazine in which each participant present the interpretation of the topic. Content will be provided, no writing skills required

Workshop and symposium.
